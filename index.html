// Methodology

Pentest Phases

01
Planning & Reconnaissance
Define scope, rules of engagement, and gather intelligence about the target without active probing. OSINT, footprinting, and passive recon.
02
Scanning & Enumeration
Actively probe the target for open ports, live hosts, services, OS, and potential attack vectors using automated and manual techniques.
03
Gaining Access
Exploit discovered vulnerabilities to gain a foothold. This may involve web exploits, network attacks, or social engineering within authorized scope.
04
Maintaining Access & Post-Exploitation
Simulate persistence mechanisms, lateral movement, and privilege escalation to understand the full impact of a real breach scenario.
05
Reporting & Remediation
Document findings, proof-of-concepts, risk ratings, and actionable remediation steps in a comprehensive penetration testing report.
